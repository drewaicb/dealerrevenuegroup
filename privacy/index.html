Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2, 2026. This policy explains what information Dealer Revenue Group, LLC ("DRG," "we," "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers information collected through our website (dealerrevenuegroup.com), our client portal (portal.dealerrevenuegroup.com), and in the course of delivering ClaimSmith™ (advertising co-op recovery), Narrative Monitor (AI visibility reporting), and Ad Expense Tracking services to business clients ("Client," "you").

2. Information we collect

We collect information in three ways:

  • Information you provide. Contact details, business information, login credentials, billing and payment details, and — for ClaimSmith clients — DMS/RO data, advertising invoices, and co-op claim documentation you upload or authorize us to access.
  • Information collected automatically. Basic site analytics (pages viewed, referring source) via Google Analytics when you visit our website.
  • Information from third-party sources. For Narrative Monitor, we query public and third-party platforms (such as AI assistants, search engines, Google Business Profile, and public review sites) for information about your business as it already appears there. We do not collect private data from these platforms — only what is publicly visible.

3. How we use information

We use information to: deliver the service you've engaged us for (submit co-op claims, generate AI-visibility reports, produce ad-spend reports); operate your portal account and billing; communicate with you about your account, invoices, and service updates; and improve our services. We do not use Client business data to train third-party AI models.

4. What we don't do

We do not sell Client data. We do not share DMS, RO, or advertising data with third parties except as required to submit an OEM co-op claim or process a payment. Any data-sharing beyond that requires your prior written consent.

5. Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store full card numbers or bank account credentials on our own systems — that information is held by Stripe under its own privacy and security practices. We retain invoice records (amounts, dates, status) needed for your billing history and our accounting.

6. Data retention

We retain Client data for as long as the service relationship is active, plus a reasonable period afterward to satisfy accounting, audit-defense, and legal obligations (for example, OEM co-op claim documentation may need to be retained to defend a submission in the event of an audit). You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us; we will retain only what we're legally required to keep.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards — encrypted connections (HTTPS), access-controlled systems, and secure third-party payment processing — to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-party services we use

We rely on a small number of trusted vendors to operate: Stripe (payments), Google Workspace / Google Drive (email and document storage), and OEM co-op portals (BrandMaster) where required to submit claims on your behalf. Each is used solely to deliver the service you've engaged us for.

9. Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at the email below. You can also opt out of non-essential email communications using the unsubscribe link in any message, or by contacting us directly.

10. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and business contacts, not individual consumers, and are not directed to children under 18.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our services after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at admin@dealerrevenuegroup.com or write to Dealer Revenue Group, LLC, PO Box 72, Belleville, WI 53508.
